jaxrts.models.SchumacherImpulseFitRk.evaluate

SchumacherImpulseFitRk.evaluate(plasma_state: PlasmaState, setup: Setup, *args, **kwargs) → Array

If sample_points is not None, generate a low-resulution :py:class`~.setup.Setup`. Calculate the instrument-function free scattering intensity with this or the given setup, interpolate it, if needed and then convolve it with the instrument function.
