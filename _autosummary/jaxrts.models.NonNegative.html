jaxrts.models.NonNegative

class jaxrts.models.NonNegative[source]

Cuts at jaxrts.setup.Setup.measured_energy = 0 eV, independently of the edge_energy given, so that only energy transfer to the electrons (down-shifted photon energies) are permitted.
